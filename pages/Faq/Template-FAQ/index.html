The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:

  • Activate your account
  • Go to the Servis Komputer Admin Website

How much do I get from each service?

Here, at Service Komputer App, we offer a great, 90% rate for each services price, regardless of any restrictions, such as volume, date of entry, etc.

What are the payment options?

The best way to transfer funds is via Bank. This secure platform ensures timely payments and a secure environment.

When do I get paid?

Our standard payment plan provides for monthly payments. At the end of each month, all accumulated funds are transfered to your account. The minimum amount of your balance should be at least IDR50.000.
